The Poisson equation also appears when solving for the pressure in incompressible flow Navier-Stokes calculations, and solving it actually accounts for much of the computational cost (as we will see later). Hence, there is a lot of interest in developing fast methods to solve linear systems of equations that arise from elliptic equations.
